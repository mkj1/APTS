Model APTS

Description
This model describes a queueing system located at an airport. Passengers will arrive and require the loading into a car. A car is on duty and will head off to find the passenger. It will then load the passenger into the car. Afterwards, the car leaves the entrance. In case the car is busy, the passenger waits for its turn at the airport entrance. If the car is idle, it waits on its own parking spot for a passenger to come.
